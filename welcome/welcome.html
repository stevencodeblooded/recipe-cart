How to Use RecipeCart

1

Go to any recipe website with an ingredient list, like the example below.

2
Open RecipeCart Extension

Click the RecipeCart icon in your browser's toolbar and click "Extract Ingredients".

3

Review the extracted ingredients, adjust quantities, and toggle between US and metric units.

4
Send to Instacart

Click "Send to Instacart" to add all ingredients to your Instacart cart.

Tips & Tricks

Save Favorite Recipes: You can save recipes for future reference by clicking the "Save Recipe" button. Access your saved recipes anytime from the popup.

Copy to Clipboard: If you prefer to use another service, you can copy all ingredients to your clipboard with one click.

Recipe Scaling: Use the 1x, 2x, and 3x buttons to scale recipes up or down depending on how many servings you need.
